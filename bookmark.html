Mac Forums 'App' Shortcut

For an app-like experience with Mac Forums, try adding a shortcut to your device's home screen (not all devices support this capability). This creates a Mac Forums icon that lives with the rest of the apps on your home screen, and it lets you quickly launch the web app with just one tap.


Add a shortcut on an Android device

  1. Go to the Mac Forums homepage.
  2. Touch your device's < Menu > button.
  3. Select More.
  4. Select Add bookmark or Bookmark page and select OK.
  5. From your device's Home screen Home button, then press the Menu button.
  6. Select Add > Shortcut > Bookmark.
  7. Find 'Mac Forums' and select it.
  8. A new Mac Forums icon appears on your home screen.

Add a shortcut on an Apple device

  1. Go to the Mac Forums homepage.
  2. Tap the center arrow icon at the bottom of the screen

    Note: On devices running iOS 4.1 or earlier, tap the +.

  3. Select Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add to confirm.
  5. A new Mac Forums icon appears on your home screen.
